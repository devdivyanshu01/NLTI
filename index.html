About Us

National Law Training Institute (NLTI) is an initiative that was started by NLSIU Students in 2022 with an aim to provide honest and comprehensive CLAT and NLSAT mentorship with accessible prices for everyone. As CLAT & NLSAT Toppers and students who got into NLS, we are cognizant of the exact circumstances that your exam preparation entails. Not too long ago, we were also getting low mock scores, getting distressed with the burden of parental and societal pressures over us. We know what it is like to give CLAT & NLSAT, to think if you are making the right decision, and to worry if you will ever be able to crack one of the top National Law Universities. Surprisingly, with the help of friends and family, we made it into the National Law School, Bangalore, the dream, the Harvard of the East. We, at NLTI, are here to give back to the CLAT & NLSAT community by being the aspirants‚Äô best friend, their guide, and their mentor. We are here to teach you concepts, help you analyze your mocks, boost your CLAT preparation, and be there by your side in all your ups and downs. One thing that we regret not having during our preparation is a mentor who can guide us throughout and at NLTI we are solving the exact problem by connecting you with the students of NLSIU directly and giving you the opportunity of having them as your mentors. Our initiative has produced over 200 selections in the Top National Law Universities. Our CLAT and AILET 2024 batch witnessed 6 students in the Top-10 ranks in AILET 2024, which is unprecedented in the industry within such a short duration. Come join NLTI and be a part of this revolution!
